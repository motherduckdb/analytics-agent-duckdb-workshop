## The Duckoffee Dataset

You'll work with **Duckoffee** — a chain of coffee venues at tech conferences across 13 cities worldwide.

<CopyBlock label="Quick check" text={`duckdb duckoffee.duckdb -c "SELECT location_name, city FROM locations;"`} />

The database has **multiple layers** — and some of them are traps:

| Layer | Tables | Watch out |
|-------|--------|-----------|
| **Raw** | raw_orders, raw_items, raw_supplies, raw_legacy_invoices | Amounts in **cents**, cryptic column names |
| **Staging** | stg_orders, stg_items, stg_supplies, stg_legacy_invoices | Clean names, euros |
| **Marts** | orders, customers, order_items | Pre-computed fields, the source of truth |
| **Dead-ends** | daily_revenue, customer_segments, order_facts | Stale, incomplete, or ambiguous |

Scale: **12k customers, 120k orders, 500k line items, 25 products, 20 venues, 12k legacy invoices.**
