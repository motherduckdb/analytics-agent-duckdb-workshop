Three tools on your machine, plus a key for the model. That's it.

3.10+. Ships with most systems — python3 --version.

Get a model key

Pick one of the two options:

  • Grab a MotherDuck-minted key at motherduck.com/minter — pre-provisioned credits, no signup hassle. Fastest path.
