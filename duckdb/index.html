About DuckDB

In-process analytical database. Think SQLite, but for analytics.

  • Built in C++, no server, no daemon
  • 1 single binary — all batteries included
  • Open source (MIT), vendor-neutral, born at CWI Amsterdam
  • Columnar vectorized engine — fast on laptop or in Lambda
  • Rich ecosystem: CLI, Python, R, Node, Java, WASM…
  • Extensions, core + community (httpfs, iceberg, spatial, delta, h3…)
